Brooks RV Park Rules & Regulations

For the mutual benefit of the park and occupants, the following rules and regulations shall remain in effect at all times - ANY violation of the following rules can result in you being vacated WITHOUT a refund.

1. Payment in FULL is expected when you check-in. For arrivals after business hours, PLEASE come to office ASAP the next day. NO site will be held WITHOUT a deposit. Lot rent must be paid in advance on the 1st when due. There is a 3 day grace period. A 10% late fee will be charged on the 4th and a $5 per day fee starting on the 5th until paid in full or a vacate notice.
2. RV's MUST be connected to the Park's sewer/ water system. Sewer lines MUST have a sewer hose seal and be kept screwed in airtight and free from leaks.
3. Lots MUST be kept free and clean of rubbish, litter, and bottles. NO storage under RV.
4. Dumpsters are provided for trash. A $20 clean up fee will be due for any trash/items left on your site. No dumping of anything but normal daily garbage. Violators will be charged a $50 clean up fee for dumping.
5. Check-in 2:00 pm. Check-out 12:00 pm. A short late check out may be available if you call.
6. Open fires permitted ONLY in grills or in contained fire pits.
7. NO shelters, etc., of any kind are to be erected without consent of Management.
8. Quiet hours ARE enforced from 10:00 P.M. until 6:00 A.M. NO loud radios or televisions.
9. Minors under age 18 MUST be accompanied by a parent or responsible adult.
10. Dogs/Cats MUST be kept on a leash at ALL times. There will be a $20/day charge if pet droppings aren't cleaned up immediately & disposed of. All dogs must be potty trained. DO NOT leave pets alone outside, tied up or in a cage. DO NOT allow pets to dig on your site. NO attack or vicious breeds allowed. Please be considerate of others & DO NOT leave dogs inside barking or walk pets on other sites. You should have your pets under your control AT ALL TIMES. If in violation of any of these rules you will be asked to leave the park.
11. NO selling or soliciting in Park without first receiving consent of Park management.
12. NO refunds for early departure. Guests agree to furnish the Park reasonable notice prior to departure. Long term guests agree to provide a 30 day notice to vacate.
13. Any occupant making sale of RV, must have new user previously approved by office; otherwise RV must be removed from the Park. User agrees that NO RV will be occupied by non-owners.
14. Drainage of water, soap, grease or other waste products into the ground or into anything other than an approved holding tank or the sewage receptacles and dump stations provided is a violation of State Law. Use a suitable holding fixture for waste water and dispose of it properly.
15. Occupants are cautioned and urged to be careful of electrical wires, objects left on the ground and the conditions of walks, drives and steps. The Park is NOT responsible for injuries to persons, property or for property lost by occupants or guests, by fire, windstorm, or other casualty. The USER assumes the risks or hazards which may be created by other occupants of the park.
16. SPEED LIMIT WILL be rigidly enforced to 10 MILES PER HOUR. You will be given one warning about speeding and the next time you will be asked leave the park. You are also responsible for your guests who visit the park.
17. Up to two (2) vehicles and camper per site. TRAILERS (FLATBED OR ENCLOSED), EQUIPMENT, OR BOATS are not allowed on certain RV sites. Management will designate an area for parking BOATS and/or EXTRA VEHICLES during your stay for a $35 additional fee. If you leave a vehicle at the park upon your departure you will be charged a storage fee. Management may grant permission for special accommodations for vehicles or boats depending on availability. ANY damage done to lot or street WILL be repaired at USER'S expense. NO loud mufflers. NO repairing vehicle/boats in the park.
18. ONLY ONE camping unit per site.
19. Occupants of this park shall be responsible for ALL guests and their conduct while in the park. ALL occupants and guests are expected to act in an orderly manner. Unreasonable conduct may result in being vacated WITHOUT refund. Children should be supervised at all times.
20. ALL rates include 2 adult occupants. There is a daily fee of $2.00 per person for additional occupants. Children under the age of 5 are exempt. Additional occupants must register at the office.
21. ALL motor vehicles MUST have State License, operated by licensed driver, on roadways designated for their use only. NO ATV 's (4-wheelers) allowed in the park. Only licensed drivers are allowed to drive motorized scooters or golf carts in the park. Indiscriminate riding back and forth is strictly forbidden.
22. The storage area is a RESTRICTED area. NO admittance without management consent.
23. NO fireworks allowed in the park.
24. Washing of cars, boats, trailers, RV's, equipment, or other vehicles is NOT allowed. There will be a $20 fine to cover utility costs and you risk eviction without a refund.
25. Minimum reservation stay is 2 days. Holiday minimum reservation stay is 3 days.
26. RV's must be 15 years old or newer. We will check the registration of your RV.
27. ALL occupants further agree to abide by any additional rules and regulations which may be shown in a conspicuous place in the park and which may, in the opinion of the owner of the park, be necessary in the operation of the park so that the same may be a desirable, attractive and proper place for persons to live.
28. Everyone has limited Wi-Fi bandwidth usage. There will be no streaming entertainment, or downloading large files, and no downloading illegal files.

30. Short-term Resident must have incoming mail sent to the Manager's office at Lot 31 unless Resident has purchased a mail box for $10 a month.
