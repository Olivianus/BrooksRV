Contact and Location

Brooks Mobile Home and RV Park is located on a 10 Acre Park in beautiful Brooks, KY between Hillview and Jefferson Memorial Forest with Gatlinburg like forested hills in the Louisville, KY metro area! Enjoy a quiet safe country setting surrounded by woods along Brooks Run Creek just 5 minutes from Louisville and Shepherdsville, and just 1 and 1/3 miles from I-65 at the Brooks exit #121! We are located in an area with no crime in our safe country environment. Phone: 502-276-6090 Ext. 1.

Directions to Louisville Rv Park

Our address is 205 Sarver Ln, Brooks, Ky; I-65 Exit 121, 1 mile West off of the Interstate.
