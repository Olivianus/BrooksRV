Available Park Home Rentals

Lot 10

Totally remodeled interior with new trim work throughout, new oak kitchen cabinets, laminate wood flooring, sand colored paint throughout, Moen gooseneck kitchen faucet with matching sprayer, new mini-blinds throughout, new bath tub surround, and nice vanity and sink. It also comes with kitchen appliances, a large pantry, and a kitchen LR combo floor-plan.There is an option for high speed internet wifi for an additional $50 a month. This would be perfect for a single person or a couple. This is a step above apartment living with no one on the other side of your walls and ceilings and you get your yard mowed for free. The rent covers your water & sewer valued at $50, lawn care valued at $30, trash pickup valued at $18, monitored security cameras, and a mail box all included in the $685 rental amount, plus we are more than half RVs, so we do plan to add more amenities because we are are becoming a Resort, so more trees, landscaping, and for enjoyment a playground, corn hole, horse shoes, etc...

The home is located in Brooks Mobile Home and RV Park which is located in a nice country setting in beautiful Brooks, Kentucky. We are just over 1 mile from the Brooks exit on I-65, and are just minutes from downtown Louisville or Shepherdsville. This is a very safe park and we have monitored security cameras recording just in case. We have well manicured lots, onsite management and maintenance, and offer Satellite TV with over 100 channels for just $38 a month, and your lawn care is free. The roads have recently been paved. The neighbors are all nice people, and this is a quiet peaceful park with absolutely no trouble makers or drug activity. Call for more information, or come by to visit and fill out an application if interested in moving into our community before we run out of available homes.
